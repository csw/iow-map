In Other Waters: Interactive Map, an interactive fog-of-war map tracker for the game In Other Waters. Track visited nodes, frontier points, and dive notes across all 11 map areas.

In Other Waters Interactive Map

In Other Waters is a narrative sci-fi diving game by Jump Over The Age. The game gives you no map while diving, so this fan-made tracker reveals each area gradually as you actually explore it. Fog of war hides everything except the path you've traveled and its immediate frontier, so you can keep your bearings without seeing the whole map at once.

Map images are from a Steam guide by Hugh Janis.
